# Advanced Payroll Tax How-To

## Where to click

Manager Portal → Advanced Payroll → Tax

## Step 1: Choose year and region

1. Set the **Year**.
2. Choose **Region** (Canada, Quebec, or United States).
3. Choose **Level** (Federal or Province/State).
4. If you choose Province/State, pick the province or state.

## Step 2: Add tax brackets

1. Enter **From**, **To**, and **Rate** for each bracket.
2. Click **Add bracket** to add another row.

## Step 3: Save configuration

1. Click **Save** to store the configuration.
2. A success message appears and the config is added to the list.

## Step 4: Manage existing configs

1. Scroll to **Existing Tax Configurations**.
2. Use the checkboxes to select one or more configs.
3. Click **Delete** (single) or **Bulk Delete** (multiple) if needed.
