# Shift Swap Approvals How-To

## Where to click

Manager Portal → Shifts & Availability → Swap Approvals

## Step 1: Review pending swaps

1. Open **Shift-Swap Approvals**.
2. Review the requester, swap details, and message.

## Step 2: Approve or deny

1. Click **Approve** or **Deny**.
2. Use **Resend e-mail** if the employee needs another notification.

## Step 3: View history

1. Toggle **Show history** to review past decisions.
