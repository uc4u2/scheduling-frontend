# W-2 How-To

## Where to click

Manager Portal → Advanced Payroll → W-2

## Step 1: Set filters

1. Choose the **Tax Year**.
2. (Optional) Filter by **Department** or **Employee**.
3. Toggle **Show archived employees** if needed.

## Step 2: Create a W-2

1. Use **Create New W-2** to open the form.
2. Review the prefilled employee and company details.
3. Save the form.

## Step 3: Review and issue

1. Use **Edit** to adjust box values if needed.
2. Click **Approve** and then **Issue** when ready.

## Step 4: Export

1. Click **Export EFW2 Batch** for the SSA text file.
2. Click **Download PDF ZIP** for all W-2 PDFs.
3. Use the PDF icon on a row to preview or download a single form.

## Step 5: Audit history

1. Click the **Audit Trail** icon to view changes.

Notes:
- W-2 is a Business-plan compliance document.
- EFW2 export is available from the header actions.
