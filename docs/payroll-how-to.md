# Payroll How-To (Manager)

This guide walks through a complete payroll cycle, from preparing time to exporting,
with the exact screens and common fields.

## Where to click

Manager Portal → Advanced Payroll → Payroll

## Step 1: Prepare time and attendance

1. Go to Manager Portal → Shifts & Availability → Time Tracking.
2. Review clock-ins/outs and fix missing punches.
3. Approve time entries for the payroll period.

### What to check

- Missing punches, long shifts, or overlapping entries.
- PTO and leave entries for the period.
- Notes added by managers or employees.

## Step 2: Open the payroll run

1. Go to Manager Portal → Advanced Payroll → Payroll.
2. Select the pay period from the dropdown.
3. Click **Preview** to load the draft payroll.

### Common payroll fields

- Pay period start/end
- Employee list with hours, overtime, tips, and deductions
- Gross, taxes, net pay totals

## Step 3: Review and adjust

1. Review gross, overtime, tips, and deductions by employee.
2. Use manager overrides if needed (bonuses, reimbursements, commissions).
3. Save changes and confirm the totals.

### Manager overrides (examples)

- Bonus
- Commission
- Tips adjustment
- Reimbursements
- Shift premiums

## Step 4: Finalize payroll

1. Click **Finalize** for the period.
2. Confirm the final totals and lock the run.

### What finalize does

- Locks the payroll period.
- Writes the audit entry for the run.
- Enables exports and downstream workflows.

## Step 5: Export or sync

1. If you use QuickBooks or Xero: click **Send to accounting**.
2. If you use Zapier workflows: click **Send payroll to Zapier** and follow the Zapier setup guide.
3. Download CSVs if needed for manual uploads.

### Export targets

- QuickBooks Online (native journal)
- Xero (native journal)
- Zapier (custom approvals, exports, payouts)
- CSV export (manual upload)

## Audit and history

Where to click:

- Manager Portal → Advanced Payroll → Payroll Audit
- Manager Portal → Advanced Payroll → Saved Payrolls

What you can see:

- Who finalized payroll and when
- Overrides applied per employee
- Export and sync status

## Troubleshooting

- If totals look wrong, re-check Time Tracking approvals.
- If an export fails, confirm the integration is connected in Settings → QuickBooks/Xero or Settings → Zapier.
