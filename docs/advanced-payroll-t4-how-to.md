# T4 How-To

## Where to click

Manager Portal → Advanced Payroll → T4

## Step 1: Set filters

1. Choose the **Tax Year**.
2. (Optional) Filter by **Department** or **Employee**.
3. Toggle **Show archived employees** if needed.

## Step 2: Generate slips

1. Click **Generate All** to create slips for the filtered set.
2. Review the summary totals that appear after generation.

## Step 3: Review and validate

1. Click **View/Edit** on a slip to review CRA boxes.
2. Click **Validate** to run CRA checks.
3. Use **Approve** and **Issue** when ready.

## Step 4: Export

1. Click **Export CRA XML** for a batch XML file.
2. Click **Download PDF ZIP** to get all PDFs.
3. You can also download a single slip PDF from its row.

## Step 5: Audit history

1. Click the **Audit Trail** icon to view changes.

Notes:
- T4 is a Business-plan compliance document.
- XML export is available after slips are issued.
