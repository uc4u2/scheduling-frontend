# Services How-To

## Where to click

Manager Portal → Services & Bookings → Services

## Step 1: Create a service

1. Click **Add Service**.
2. Fill the required fields:
   - Name
   - Category
   - Duration (minutes)
   - Base price
3. Add a description and optional images.
4. Click **Save**.

## Step 2: Edit or archive

1. Open a service row.
2. Update price, duration, or description.
3. Toggle **Active** to archive/unarchive.
4. Click **Save**.

## Step 3: Assign staff and resources

1. Open the service.
2. Assign eligible staff.
3. Add required resources (rooms, chairs, equipment).
4. Save changes.
