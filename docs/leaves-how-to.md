# Leave Requests How-To

## Where to click

Manager Portal → Shifts & Availability → Leaves

## Step 1: Filter requests

1. Choose a **Status** filter (Pending, Approved, Rejected).

## Step 2: Review details

1. Review recruiter name, shift date/time, leave type/subtype.
2. Check paid/unpaid status and override hours.

## Step 3: Approve or reject

1. Add a **Comment** if needed.
2. Click **Approve** or **Reject**.
