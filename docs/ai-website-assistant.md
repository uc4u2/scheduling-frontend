# AI Website Assistant (Manager)

Use the AI Website Assistant to draft on-brand copy for your website sections,
FAQ ideas, and SEO suggestions.

## Where to find it

Manager Portal → Website & Pages → Visual Site Builder → Help → AI Website Assistant

## Step-by-step

1. Open the Visual Site Builder.
2. Click **Help** (top of the builder).
3. Scroll to **AI Website Assistant**.
4. Describe your business in 1–2 sentences.
   - Example: "Brow & lash studio in Toronto with 5 chairs and online booking."
5. Click **Generate copy**.
6. Review the draft blocks (Hero, Sections, FAQ, SEO Suggestions).
7. Copy the parts you like and paste them into your page sections or SEO fields.
8. Save Draft, then Publish when you are ready to go live.

## What it generates

- **Hero copy**: headline, subtitle, and CTAs.
- **Section copy**: short paragraphs you can drop into sections.
- **FAQ ideas**: question + answer suggestions.
- **SEO suggestions**: title + meta description starting points.

## Tips for better results

- Include your location, specialty, and audience.
- Mention the top 1–2 services you want to promote.
- Use the AI output as a draft; edit for accuracy and tone.

## Notes

- The AI does **not** publish changes automatically.
- Always click **Publish** to make changes live.
