# Inline Site Editor How-To

## Where to click

Manager Portal → Website & Pages → Inline Site Editor

## Step 1: Open the editor

1. Open **Inline Site Editor** from Website & Pages.
2. Choose a page tab (Home, Services, About, etc.).

## Step 2: Preview your page

1. Scroll the live preview to confirm layout and content.
2. Switch tabs to review other pages.

## Step 3: Change the theme (optional)

1. Click **Change Theme** to rotate through themes.
2. Review the preview to confirm the look.

## Step 4: Edit in the full builder (optional)

1. Click **Edit Site** to open the full Visual Site Builder.
2. Make changes there and return when finished.

## Step 5: Publish

1. Click **Publish** to make draft changes live.
2. A draft badge appears when changes are pending.
