# Time Tracking How-To (Manager)

Use Time Tracking to review hours before payroll.

## Where to click

Manager Portal → Shifts & Availability → Time Tracking

## Step 1: Review the period

1. Choose the date range or pay period.
2. Scan for missing punches or unusually long shifts.

## Step 2: Fix issues

1. Open a time entry to edit start/end times.
2. Add manager notes if needed.
3. Save the correction.

## Step 3: Approve time

1. Select entries (or use **Approve all** if available).
2. Confirm approval to lock the period for payroll.

## Step 4: Check anomalies

1. Go to Manager Portal → Shifts & Availability → Fraud / Anomalies.
2. Review flagged items and investigate exceptions.

## Tips

- Approve time before opening payroll previews.
- Use manager notes to explain corrections for audits.
