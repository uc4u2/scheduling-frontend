# Payroll Invoices How-To

## Where to click

Manager Portal → Advanced Payroll → Invoices

## Step 1: Choose invoice type

1. Use the tabs to choose:
   - **Payroll billing (auto from payroll)**
   - **Simple / manual invoices**

## Step 2: Create a new invoice

1. Click **New payroll billing invoice** or **New simple invoice**.
2. Select or add a **Recipient**.
3. Set **Issue date** and **Due date**.
4. For payroll billing invoices, set **Period start** and **Period end**.
5. Click **Save**.

## Step 3: Review and manage

1. Use the list to review status, totals, and due dates.
2. Use **Actions** to edit, send, or delete an invoice.

Notes:
- Payroll billing invoices are generated from finalized payrolls.
- Simple invoices use your custom line items.
