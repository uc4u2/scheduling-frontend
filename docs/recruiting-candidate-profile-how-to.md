# Candidate Profile How-To

## Where to click

Manager Portal → Overview → Candidate Profile

Tip: You can also open a candidate from **Job Postings → Applications → Open candidate profile**.

## Step 1: Review profile details

1. Open the profile to see personal info, job info, and online links.
2. Use **Interview & Documents** to access meeting links and resume downloads.

## Step 2: Manage reminders

1. Fill **Title**, **Notify at**, and **Message**.
2. Click **Save reminder**.
3. Use **Snooze** or **Cancel** as needed.

## Step 3: Request documents (optional)

1. Click **Request documents**.
2. Enter a subject and message.
3. Copy the upload link for the candidate.

## Step 4: Update candidate status

1. Scroll to **Update Details**.
2. Edit name, contact info, notes, and **Status**.
3. Click **Save Changes**.

## Step 5: Conversion to employee (optional)

1. Review the conversion status.
2. Click **Request manager approval** if you need to convert the candidate to an employee profile.

Notes:
- Some actions are read-only unless you have HR onboarding permissions.
- Resume downloads may be blocked until scanning finishes.
