# Payments and Orders How-To

## Where to click

Manager Portal → Services & Bookings → Payments

## Step 1: Review payments

1. Filter by date range or status.
2. Open a payment to see details.
3. Issue refunds if available.

## Step 2: Review product orders

1. Switch to Product Orders view if available.
2. Open an order to see items and totals.
3. Update status if needed.
