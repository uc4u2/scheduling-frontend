# Employee Management How-To

## Company Profile

1. Open **Company Profile**.
2. Update your business name, address, timezone, and contact info.
3. Click **Save**.

## Employee Profiles

1. Open **Employee Profiles**.
2. Search or filter to find a team member.
3. Click the employee to open their profile.
4. Update role, permissions, availability, or contact details.
5. Click **Save**.

## Add Team Member

1. Open **Add team member**.
2. Enter name, email, and role.
3. Choose access level and permissions.
4. Click **Send Invite** (or **Save** if invite is optional).
