# Payroll Audit How-To

## Where to click

Manager Portal → Advanced Payroll → Payroll Audit

## Step 1: Filter the audit log

1. Filter by **Department** or **Employee**.
2. Set **Region** if needed.
3. Choose **Start date** and **End date**.
4. Toggle **Overwrites only** to see overrides.
5. Click **Refresh**.

## Step 2: Review entries

1. Review the period, action, and finalized by columns.
2. Click **View snapshot** to see the stored payroll snapshot and diff.
3. Click **PDF** to download the audit PDF.

## Step 3: Page through results

1. Use **Page size** and the pagination controls to move through results.

Notes:
- Payroll Audit is available on Pro and Business plans.
