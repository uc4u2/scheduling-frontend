# Payroll Audit and Invoices

Two areas cover payroll audit logs and billing records.

## Payroll Audit (Advanced Payroll)

Where to click: Manager Portal → Advanced Payroll → Payroll Audit

Use this view to review payroll actions, edits, and finalized runs. It is the primary audit log for payroll operations.

## Payroll Audit History (Overview shortcut)

Where to click: Manager Portal → Overview → Payroll Audit History

This is a quick link to audit history and recent payroll events.

## Invoices

Where to click: Manager Portal → Advanced Payroll → Invoices

Use this view to review payroll-related invoices and billing records.
