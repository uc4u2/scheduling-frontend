# Job Postings How-To

## Where to click

Manager Portal → Overview → Job Postings

## Step 1: Create a job

1. Click **Create job**.
2. Fill in title, slug, description, and role details.
3. Save the job (it stays in **Draft** until published).

## Step 2: Publish or close

1. Use the **Publish** icon to make a draft job live.
2. Use the **Close** icon to stop new applications.

## Step 3: Share the public link

1. Use **Copy public link** or **Open public link**.
2. Only **Published** jobs appear on the public page.

## Step 4: Review applications

1. Click the **Applications** icon on a job row.
2. Review applicants, update stages, and export CSV if needed.

Notes:
- Job Postings require Pro plan or higher.
- Draft jobs can be deleted only if there are no applications.
