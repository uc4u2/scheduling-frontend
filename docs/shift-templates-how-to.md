# Shift Templates How-To

## Where to click

Manager Portal → Services & Bookings → Shift Templates

## Step 1: Create a template

1. Click **Add Template**.
2. Set:
   - Name
   - Day of week
   - Start / End time
   - Role or department (if available)
3. Click **Save**.

## Step 2: Assign to employees

1. Open the template.
2. Select employees to assign.
3. Save changes.
