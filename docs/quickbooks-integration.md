# QuickBooks Integration

Schedulaa connects to QuickBooks Online so you can export payroll and revenue summaries
without manual spreadsheets.

## Where to find it
- Manager Dashboard -> Settings -> QuickBooks

## Connect QuickBooks
1) Open the **QuickBooks** tab in Settings.
2) Click **Connect QuickBooks** and finish the Intuit consent flow.
3) Confirm the status shows your company name and an active connection.

## Map accounts
1) In **Payroll account mapping**, match Schedulaa payroll buckets (wages, taxes, tips, net pay)
   to the correct QuickBooks accounts.
2) Save each mapping row.
3) If you use departments or locations, map them to QuickBooks classes/locations.

## Export payroll
1) Go to **Payroll**.
2) Review the payroll summary and click **Send to accounting**.
3) Choose **QuickBooks** and confirm the export.

## Troubleshooting
- If export fails, return to Settings -> QuickBooks and click **Refresh status**.
- Reconnect if tokens expired.

## Permissions
- Manager access is required to connect or export to QuickBooks.
