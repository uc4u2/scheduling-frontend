# Fraud & Anomalies How-To

## Where to click

Manager Portal → Shifts & Availability → Fraud / Anomalies

## Step 1: Set the date range

1. Choose **From** and **To** dates.
2. Select an **Anomaly type** (Any, New device, New location, Multi-IP, Outside trusted IP).
3. Click **Refresh**.

## Step 2: Review the summary

1. Check **Anomaly events**, **Shifts in range**, and **Shifts with anomalies**.
2. Review **Outside trusted IPs** count.

## Step 3: Review offenders

1. Use **Top high-risk days** to spot spikes.
2. Review **Top offenders** for repeated issues.

## Step 4: Review events

1. Scroll to **Anomaly events** to see each clock-in/out.
2. Use the **Flags** chips to identify the reason.

Notes:
- Use Trusted IPs in Company Profile to reduce false positives.
- The Help panel explains the risk scoring rules.
