# Marketing Campaigns How-To

## Where to click

Manager Portal → Services & Bookings → Marketing / Campaigns

## Step 1: Create a campaign

1. Click **New Campaign**.
2. Set a campaign name and date range.
3. Choose the audience (all clients or a segment).
4. Add the promotion details (offer, code, or message).
5. Click **Save**.

## Step 2: Activate or pause

1. Open a campaign.
2. Toggle **Active** to pause or resume.
3. Save changes.

## Step 3: Review performance

1. Open the campaign.
2. Review engagement or redemption metrics if available.

## Common uses

- Promo codes for slow weeks
- Rebooking offers
- New service launches

## Campaign library (step-by-step)

Use these prebuilt campaigns to improve sales and retention.

### 1) Broadcast
Goal: announce news or a limited-time offer to a broad audience.
Steps:
1. Choose **Broadcast**.
2. Pick the audience (all clients or a segment).
3. Add a short offer + CTA.
4. Preview, then send.

### 2) Win-back
Goal: re-engage clients who have not booked recently.
Steps:
1. Choose **Win-back**.
2. Review the pre-filtered audience.
3. Add a simple offer (e.g., 10% off).
4. Preview, then send.

### 3) Skipped Rebook
Goal: follow up with clients who did not rebook.
Steps:
1. Choose **Skipped Rebook**.
2. Review the suggested list.
3. Add a friendly reminder + booking link.
4. Preview, then send.

### 4) VIP
Goal: reward top clients and increase repeat visits.
Steps:
1. Choose **VIP**.
2. Review the VIP segment.
3. Add a perk (priority slots, loyalty reward).
4. Preview, then send.

### 5) Anniversary
Goal: celebrate client milestones and boost retention.
Steps:
1. Choose **Anniversary**.
2. Confirm the milestone window.
3. Add a short thank‑you + offer.
4. Preview, then send.

### 6) New Service Launch
Goal: promote a new service to the right clients.
Steps:
1. Choose **New Service Launch**.
2. Select the service and target segment.
3. Add the booking CTA.
4. Preview, then send.

### 7) No‑Show Recovery
Goal: recover revenue and reduce future no‑shows.
Steps:
1. Choose **No‑Show Recovery**.
2. Review the affected clients.
3. Add a policy reminder or incentive.
4. Preview, then send.

### 8) Add‑on Upsell
Goal: increase order value with add‑ons.
Steps:
1. Choose **Add‑on Upsell**.
2. Pick the add‑on and target segment.
3. Add a short benefit statement.
4. Preview, then send.
