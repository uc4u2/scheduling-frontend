# Marketing Campaigns How-To

## Where to click

Manager Portal → Services & Bookings → Marketing / Campaigns

## Step 1: Create a campaign

1. Click **New Campaign**.
2. Set a campaign name and date range.
3. Choose the audience (all clients or a segment).
4. Add the promotion details (offer, code, or message).
5. Click **Save**.

## Step 2: Activate or pause

1. Open a campaign.
2. Toggle **Active** to pause or resume.
3. Save changes.

## Step 3: Review performance

1. Open the campaign.
2. Review engagement or redemption metrics if available.

## Common uses

- Promo codes for slow weeks
- Rebooking offers
- New service launches
